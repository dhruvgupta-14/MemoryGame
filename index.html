Rules For Game

1.Shuffle and lay cards face down in a grid.

2.Flip two cards each turn; match pairs to keep them and take another turn. If no match, flip them back and count a move.

3.Remember card positions to find pairs.

4.Game ends when all pairs are found.Fewer moves indicate a better performance.

5.You have two minutes to complete the game and a maximum of 40 moves. The game ends if the time runs out or you reach 40 moves.
